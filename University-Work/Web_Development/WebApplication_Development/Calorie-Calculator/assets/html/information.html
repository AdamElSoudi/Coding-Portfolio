What
are
Calories?

It is important to understand what calories are before starting to keep track on them. Calories are how we measure energy in foods and drinks. Our bodies need energy to work and when we consume more calories than we use up, our bodies store the excess calories as fat around our bodies and in turn we eventually gain weight.

What
are
Proteins?

Proteins are a critical part in our diets, whether you are trying to build muscles or not, they are still an important amino acid that your body needs. Proteins mostly work in the cells and help the structure and function of the bodies tissues and organs.

Why are they
Important?

Trying to achieve a healthier lifestyle can be difficult and this website is here to make that journey a bit easier. Allowing you to add the ingredients in your meals and get a total calorie count and protein count is very benefitial and an important part to reach your goals whether that is losing weight or gaining weight. Depending on your goal, you either have to eat more calories than you burn or vice versa and the when you know how many calories you are burning each day, the next step is to figure out how many calories you are consuming.
