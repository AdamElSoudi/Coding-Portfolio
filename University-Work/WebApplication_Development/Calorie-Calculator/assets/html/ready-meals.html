Here are some easy & healthy premade meals and their ingredients:

Chicken & Rice

  • 150g of chicken breast

Total Calories: Total Protein:
430 kcal 38g

Salmon & Rice

  • 200g of cooked rice

538 kcal 31g

Tuna & Pasta

  • 130g of tuna

  • 100g of Creme fraiche (you get to decide the flavour)

Total Calories: Total Protein:
577 kcal 38g
